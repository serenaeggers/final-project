About

Serena Eggers is an artist, illustrator, and web designer, originally from New York City.

She is currently attending Harvard University, where she is majoring in Visual and Environmental Studies (otherwise known as Art) and minoring in English. She expects to graduate in 2017.

This is her second semester serving as head of the Art Board of the Harvard Lampoon.

She enjoys art, books, computer coding, and long walks on the beach.
